Job description
Description

Role Summary:

We are seeking a Network Engineer to join our team. You will be a member of the Global Network Services team responsible for ensuring a highly available global network to enable Peloton’s growing global presence. This includes engineering, deploying, maintaining, and supporting the global wired and wireless data network, voice systems, switches, routers, firewalls, site-to-site IPsec tunnels and client VPN. You will collaborate with our DevOps & AV team on several projects and initiatives in addition to partnering with our Cyber Security team to

implement, maintain and support security principles and best practices for our internal user community to align with the best-in-class security services.

Responsibilities:

•Deploy and support a complex global network environment

•Ensure network stability and performance through operational

excellence and proactive maintenance

•Troubleshoot network problems; schedule upgrades; perform

network analysis, root cause analysis and optimization

•Ensure uptime and monitoring of all corporate network

infrastructure and circuits internationally

•Secure the network infrastructure by partnering with the

Information Security team to establish and enforce policies;

define and monitor access

•Manage network upgrades, expansions, and new implementation

projects

•Evaluate partner solution portfolio roadmaps and new technologies

for potential deployment across Peloton’s entire footprint of

retail stores, warehouses, call centers, and offices globally.

•Collaborate on and document standard operating procedures

•Ensure that network documentation and diagrams are up to date.

•Provide troubleshooting guidance and training for Tier 1 IT support

•Provide 24x7 Tier 3 IT Network Support

Requirements:

•Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Computer Science, or a related

field

•One or more industry leading certifications preferred such as

•Experience with Cisco, Meraki, Aruba Wireless and Palo Alto

Networks.

environments

•Experience with protocols and standards common in media

delivery (e.g., HLS, MPEG-DASH, RTMP, SRT, RTP, IGMP)

•Understanding of QoS, traffic shaping, and network performance
